Game facts

Here we got some facts about the game.For example instructions and what it is about. Press the correct word that belongs to the correct color above the words. You start with 10 points. You lose when you're down to -10 points. If you press the correct button you earn 10 points. If you press the wrong button you lose 5points. You have 2 seconds to press the correct button. If the time runs out you lose 10 points. When the songs ends the game is over. Then you can see how much score you have.You can increase your score for each time. Have fun!
